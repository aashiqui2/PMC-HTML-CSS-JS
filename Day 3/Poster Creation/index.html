User Experience Research

UX (user experience) research is the systematic study of target users and their requirements, to add realistic contexts and insights to design processes.UX researchers adopt various methods to uncover problems and design opportunities. Doing so, they reveal valuable information which can be fed into the design process.

UX Research is about Finding Insights to Guide Successful Designs

UX researchers often begin with qualitative measures, to determine users’ motivations and needs. Later,they might use quantitative measures to test their results. To do UX research well, you must take a structured approach when you gather data from your users. It’s vital to use methods that

  • Are right for the purpose of your research
  • Will give you the clearest information. Then, you can interpret your findings so you can build valuable insights into your design.

Use UX Research Methods throughout Development

  • Discover – Determine what is relevant for users.
  • Explore – Examine how to address all users’ needs.
  • Test – Evaluate your designs.
  • Listen – Put issues in perspective, find any new problems and notice trends.
